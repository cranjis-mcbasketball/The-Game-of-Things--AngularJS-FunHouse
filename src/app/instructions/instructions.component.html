Instructions

The Concept

"The Game of Things" is intended to be a five-person game: there is one judge and four contestants at any given time, with judges rotating amongst the five players

The Process

[1] A random prompt is selected upon clicking "Start New Game!", and from then on after "Next Round" is clicked. A full game has 10 rounds.

[2] Each player receives 6 random beginnings, 6 random actions, and 6 random explanations per round and must come up with a statement using one of each in response to the prompt.

[3] After all four players have submitted their responses, the judge will have two jobs:
1. To rank the responses in order from favorite to least favorite
2. Guess who came up with each answer

[4] After the judge submits their feedback, it will render to the screen and points will be applied accordingly

[Points System]
Correctly guessing the author of an answer: 1pt
Winning top answer: 5pts

[Tips]
If sharing a computer, do not submit forms for Players 1, 2, 3, and 4 in that order; submit them in a random order so the judge doesn't know wrote what prior to guessing
